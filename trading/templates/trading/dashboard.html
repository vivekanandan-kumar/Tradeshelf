What are Camarilla Levels?

Camarilla levels are advanced pivot points derived from the previous day's trading range. They help identify key intraday support and resistance levels.

  • • H3/H4: Resistance levels for selling opportunities
  • • L3/L4: Support levels for buying opportunities
  • • Breakouts: H4 and L4 indicate strong breakout levels

Trading Strategy

Use Camarilla levels to identify potential reversal points and breakout opportunities. Combine with other technical indicators for confirmation.

Whole Number Theory

Psychological whole numbers (like 100, 150, 200) often act as strong support and resistance levels due to human psychology and round number bias.

  • • Identify stocks near whole number highs/lows
  • • Use for entry and exit positioning
  • • Combine with volume analysis for confirmation

Risk Management

Always use proper position sizing and stop losses. The platform provides calculated stop loss and target levels for each trading setup.
